Sneaker News

We’re only a day away from the official launch of Nike’s first ever retail release featuring that futuristic self-lacing technology with the Nike HyperAdapt 1.0. We already know that the HyperAdapt will be a rather limited offering for $720 USD, and today we get a full look at the futuristic silhouette’s unique packaging courtesy of Nike News.The box includes an AC adapter, charging pucks for the internal motor that ignites the automatic lacing system, and a handy quick-start manual so you’ll know how to use the brand new tech. Check out more detailed Nike HyperAdapt 1.0 shots below and know that you’ll be able to pick up the debut Black/White Blue Lagoon colorway tomorrow, December 1st at Nike Soho and the Nike+ Clubhouse at 45 Grand Street in New York City. Stay tuned for more release date updates right here on Sneaker News.
